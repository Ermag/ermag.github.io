Alter - Twitch Extension

Description

  • Webstroy Ltd.

  • twitch.tv

  • 2019

Alter connects streamers and viewers by providing live rewards, achievements, and recognition. By watching, viewers will earn loyalty points (followers and subscribers receive more). Loyalty points can be used by viewers to claim rewards, play sounds, show memes on stream or climb the ladder.

Technologies

  • HTML
  • SASS
  • Vue.js
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB
