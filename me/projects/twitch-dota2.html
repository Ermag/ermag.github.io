Dota 2 Overlay - Twitch Extension

Description

  • Webstroy Ltd.

  • twitch.tv

  • 2018

Interactive video overlay extension for twitch streamers that presents the following information to their viewers:
  - Recent games played
  - In-depth details about past matches
  - Overall game performance
  - Heroes played and their success rate
  - List of records with filters

Technologies

  • HTML5
  • Vue.js
  • Webpack
  • PHP (Slim)
